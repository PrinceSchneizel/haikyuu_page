Let me introduce you to Haikyuu

Haikyu!! (ハイキュー!!, Haikyū!!, from the kanji 排球 "volleyball") is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Haruichi Furudate. The story follows Shoyo Hinata, a boy determined to become a great volleyball player despite his small stature. It was serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump from February 2012 to July 2020, with its chapters collected in forty-five tankōbon volumes.

Top 4 Spikers

The following are the 4 best high school ace players in Japan as of 2012-2013.

Ushijima Wakatoshi

Wakatoshi Ushijima (Japanese: 牛島 若利 , Ushijima Wakatoshi) is a third year student at Shiratorizawa Academy. He is the captain of the boys' volleyball team. He plays as a wing spiker and is the team's ace. He was ranked among the country's top three aces.

Sakusa Kiyoomi

Kiyoomi Sakusa (Japanese: 佐久早 聖臣 , Sakusa Kiyoomi) was the ace spiker for Itachiyama Institute, a heavy favorite to win the nationals. He was one of the top three aces of the country, along with Kiryū and Ushijima, and the only second-year among them.

Bokuto Koutarou

Kōtarō Bokuto (Japanese: 木兎 光太郎 Bokuto Kōtarō) was a third-year student at Fukurōdani Academy. He played on the volleyball team as the ace and captain. He was acknowledged as one of the top five aces in the country, barely missing the top three.

Ojiro Aran

Aran Ojiro (Japanese: 尾白 アラン Ojiro Aran), also romanized as Alan Ojiro, is a third year student at Inarizaki High School. He plays on the volleyball team as a wing spiker and the ace. He is known as one of the top five aces in Japan.
